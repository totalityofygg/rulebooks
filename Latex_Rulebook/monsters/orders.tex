\mysection{Orders}{monster-orders}

Monsters are categorized into various Orders, each with one or more \mylink{Traits}{monster-traits} associated with it (see below).  The Traits are common to all the species beneath the Order.

In addition to these Orders are two specific Genera - \mylink{Dragons}{monster-dragons} and \mylink{Elementals}{monster-elementals} - that are dealt with separately.
